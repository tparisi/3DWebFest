submit

Are you developing an amazing, engaging or shocking 3D web site? Whether your site is about gaming, entertainment, architecture, or anything else-- and whether it's commercial, non-profit or just for fun-- if your web site delivers an artistic real time 3D experience, we encourage you to apply to be a presenter in the 3D Web Fest. If your submission is selected, you’ll have 1 to 3 minutes to do a live demonstration of your web site in front of celebrity guest judges and a live audience. You can bring your own musical accompaniment – or partner with a talented musician or DJ at one of our 3D Web Fest Mixers that will run every few weeks up to the time of the event.

Our Selection Committee will be reviewing submissions in March and April and announcing the selections in late April. The committee will be reviewing submissions using several criteria:

Art Direction

Is the art thought-provoking? Innovative? Unique? Cohesive? Attention will be paid to the use of 3D modeling, texture painting, shaders, procedural geometry.

Animation

We will be evaluating the use of 3D and 2D animation, including key frames, skinning, morphs, procedural textures, shader-based animation.

Narrative

Narrative can really make your work stand out. Does the piece tell a story? Inspire discussion? Raise questions?

Sound Design

Web browsers don't just do graphics; they do audio, too. Works that make good use of sound will be given special consideration.

Interaction Design

The web is an interactive medium, so your piece should be interactive. We have an eye out for works that feature superior interaction design.

Mobile

Though not a requirement, 3D web sites designed specifically for mobile, or that work just as well on mobile devices as they do on PCs will be favored. Consider the possibilities of real time “audience participation”.

Virtual Reality

We are looking for a few immersive pieces that incorporate virtual reality technology such as Oculus Rift or Google Cardboard, and novel input technology like the Leap Motion motion controller.

Hardware/Software Systems

While we would love for all the pieces featured in the 3D Web Fest to run on every platform, we are also quite OK with custom hardware configurations. If your demo requires special hardware to do something truly ground-breaking, by all means go for it! We will give it special consideration.

Installation/Live Performance

We are looking for some pieces that blur the lines between the online and real worlds. We would love to see submissions that incorporate fixed installations and a live performance with multiple participants.
